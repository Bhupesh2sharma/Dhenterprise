About Us

DH Enterprises is a leading Geotechnical and Civil Engineering firm with over 8 years of experience. We specialize in the supply and execution of comprehensive geotechnical and civil engineering projects across India. Under the adept leadership of our Managing Director, Mr. Dilwar Hussain, our dedicated and dynamic team strives to deliver top-notch services with unwavering commitment to quality and innovation.

  • Expertise in Slope Stabilization, Hydropower Projects, and Tunneling.
  • Utilizing the latest techniques and technologies for reliable and timely solutions.
  • Committed to meeting clients' needs with sincerity and excellence.

Our goal is to define quality in every project we undertake, making us one of the preferred companies for our clients. We offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients, including geotechnical field investigations, topographical surveys, geophysical investigations, geological studies, project management, and non-destructive testing on concrete.
